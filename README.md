Hi! I am Swetan Sinha. I have made this repo in order to investigate the orthongonal orientation of Turing Patterns when spatial heterogeneity is introduced to the diffusion constant. 

I am working in this repo in order to obtain graphs for my paper, which I am currently writing. I am quantifying the orientation of the stripes by calculating the angle the nabla function of the 
concentration feild makes with the diffusion gradient itself. I have seen that the angle is indeed close to 90 degrees, which helps me show that the concentration feild is orthogonal to the gradient. 
I am also investigating ImageJ and its applications here. 

Of course, perpendicular and orthogonal are not the same, and they should not be thought of as similiar, but based on my knowledge and understanding, showing the perpedicular angle supports the case for
orthongonality. 

This project is very fun -I have learned a lot of physics and calculus, making the reaction-diffusion equation one of my favorites (close second to F=ma)
